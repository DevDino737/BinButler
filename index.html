FAQ

Do I need to be home?

No — we handle everything for you.

What days do we come?

Sunday evening, Monday morning, Tuesday evening, Wednesday morning.

What bins do we bring to the curb?

We bring all of your bins — trash, yard waste, and recycling. (We don't move trash bags from inside to outside, we just move the bins to the curb and back!)

How do I pay?

We accept cash and PayPal. You will be informed how to pay after signing up via email or text.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, absolutely. There are no contracts, so you can pause or cancel service whenever you like.

What should I call for?

Calling can be used for signing up, Canceling or for any questions and more!
