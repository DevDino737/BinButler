ABOUT BINBUTLER

Simple local service, built with care.

Hi! I’m Wyatt, a 15-year-old from St. Louis who started BinButler to make trash day easier for neighbors. Right now, we only serve O'Fallon and Saint Peters. As a Christian company, our faith shapes how we work: the way we treat people, how we communicate, and the high standards we hold ourselves to every week! We are so grateful for every neighbor who helps make BinButler what it is today.

Simple guidelines that help service go smoothly.

Easy Access

Please make sure garages, gates, or bin areas can be reached safely.

Pets Secured

Keep pets safely inside or secured during service visits.

Bins Not Overloaded

Overfilled bins can be unsafe to move, and the trash truck crew may skip them, so please keep them manageable.

Bins Only

We only move trash, recycling, and yard waste bins. We do not move loose trash bags.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be home?

No — we handle everything for you.

What bins do we bring to the curb?

We bring all of your bins — trash, yard waste, and recycling. We only move bins, not loose trash bags. We only move residential bins only.

How do I pay?

We accept cash and PayPal. You will be informed how to pay after signing up via email or text.

What should I call for?

Calling can be used for signing up, canceling, or for any questions and more!
